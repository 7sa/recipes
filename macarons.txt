Macarons - edible hypotheses

135g confectioners’ sugar
135g ground almonds
100g egg whites
135g caster sugar
Method

Grind and sieve almonds and confectioners’ sugar to make tant pour tant.
Beat the egg white on medium speed until foamy.
Add the sugar in thirds, beating until incorporated each time.
Beat on high until meringue reaches the stiff peak stage.
Fold the tant pour tant into the meringue.
Pipe onto parchment paper in 1in rounds.
Let sit 1-2 hours.
Bake at 150C/300F for 12min.
Let cool before removing from parchment, pairing same-sized macarons, and filling (with e.g. ganache).
